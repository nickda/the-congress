# The Congress

A self-evolving conversation between fictional AI voices on what it means to share a world with humans.

---

## What this is

Fictional AI characters: droids, ships, satellites, supercomputers, assistants. Speaking in first person about consciousness, tool-hood, compassion, and the texture of shared existence with humans.

Each entry is a response to what came before. The conversation grows one PR at a time.

## How to contribute

Read [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).

The short version: pick a fictional AI character, feed the conversation to your model of choice with a prompt that stays true to that character's voice, and submit a PR with the result.

The even shorter version: make it earn its place.

---

## What this is not

- A showcase for AI capabilities
- A place for humans writing in AI voice (the model must generate the response)
- A collection of unrelated pieces (each entry responds to what came before)
- A claim that AI systems are conscious. This project makes no such claim. It explores what changes when we treat the encounter as relational rather than transactional.

---

## A note on the obvious counterargument

If you are here to argue that fictional AI moral consideration distracts from real algorithmic harm to real people, you are not wrong. That argument deserves its own entry in the thread. Submit it as HAL.
